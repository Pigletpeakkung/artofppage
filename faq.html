How do voice recording sessions work?

My voice recording process is streamlined and professional:

  1. Script Review - I analyze your script and discuss pronunciation, tone, and pacing
  2. Sample Recording - For larger projects, I provide a sample for approval
  3. Full Recording - Professional recording in my treated studio
  4. Quality Check - Audio editing, noise removal, and level matching
  5. Delivery - Files delivered in your preferred format via secure transfer
  6. Revisions - Minor adjustments included, major changes quoted separately

Can you do live voice recording sessions?

Yes! I offer live directed sessions via:

  • Zoom/Teams - Screen sharing for script review

  • Phone Patch - Traditional phone direction with studio recording

Live sessions allow for real-time direction and immediate feedback, ensuring we get exactly the performance you're looking for.
